Our Aged Care Services Include

Bathing, hygiene, and grooming

Service to help you maintain your personal hygiene and grooming standards.

Nursing Services

A registered Nurse visits you at home to help you treat and monitor medical conditions and to improve your health and wellbeing.

Assistance with Daily Life

Service to help you with daily life activities and to Improve living arrangements.

Meals and food preparation

Service Meals and food preparation to help ensure that you continue to eat well.

Help with impairments or continence

Service Help with impairments or continence to help manage particular conditions and maximise your independence at home.

Cleaning, laundry, and other chores

Service Cleaning, laundry, and other chores to help you keep your home clean and liveable.

Home or garden maintenance

Service Home or garden maintenance to help you keep your home and garden in a safe condition.

Assistive Technology and Home Modifications

Service Changes to your home to help increase or maintain your ability to move around your home safely and independently.

Aids to stay independent

Service Aids to stay independent to help you with mobility, communication, reading, and personal care limitations.

Transport

Service Transport to get you out and about to appointments and community activities.

Social outings, groups and visitors

Service Social outings, groups and visitors to help you remain social and interact with your community.

Day/overnight respite

Service Day/overnight respite to support you and your carer by giving you both a break for a short period of time.
