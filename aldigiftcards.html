How this works

You can send us an email to admin@abhacareservices.com.au stating your intention to buy ALDI gift cards from us and the amount you want to buy. ALDI gift cards are available for purchase in denominations of $20 AUD, $50 AUD, $100 AUD or any value up to $1000 AUD. You can order multiple gift cards up to $3000 AUD per customer per day. In the email, please also state your delivery address. Then we calculate postage costs and will reply to your email with a Bitcoin Lightning invoice that expires within 24 hours. Once you made the payment, we will go to an ALDI store, buy your gift cards and activate them and then will post them to your address with Audtralia Post.

We also offer local pick up if you are willing to travel to Western Sydney. If you choose local pick up, you will save the postage costs. Please just mention in your email that you want local pick up and we will send you the pick up address after you made your payment.

Before purchase, please read ALDI Gift Cards FAQs here: https://www.aldi.com.au/about-aldi/aldi-gift-cards/gift-cards-faqs/ and also read ALDI Gift Cards Terms & Conditions of Use here: https://www.aldi.com.au/aldi-gift-cards-terms-of-use/
