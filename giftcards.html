How this works

You can send us an email to admin@abhacareservices.com.au stating your intention to buy ALDI gift cards or COSTCO shop cards from us and the amount you want to buy. ALDI gift cards and COSTCO shop cards are available for purchase in denominations of $25 AUD, $50 AUD, $100 AUD or any value up to $1000 AUD. You can order multiple gift cards or shop cards up to $3000 AUD per customer per day. In the email, please also state your delivery address and whether you want to pay with Lightning or on-chain Bitcoin. Then we calculate postage costs and will reply to your email with a Bitcoin Lightning invoice or an on-chain address. This quote will expire in 1 hour so you need to be ready for our reply and pay quickly. You can leave your mobile number in your email and ask us to send you an sms when we sending you your email quote. Once you made the payment, we will go to an ALDI store or COSTCO warehouse, buy your gift cards or shop cards, and activate them and then will post them to your address with Australia Post. We can post to anywhere in Australia.

We also offer local pick up if you are willing to travel to Western Sydney. If you choose local pick up, you will save the postage costs. Please just mention in your email that you want local pick up and we will send you the pick up address and will arrange a pick up time after you made your payment.

Before purchase, please read ALDI Gift Cards FAQs here: https://www.aldi.com.au/about-aldi/aldi-gift-cards/gift-cards-faqs/ and also read ALDI Gift Cards Terms & Conditions of Use here: https://www.aldi.com.au/aldi-gift-cards-terms-of-use/ and also read COSTCO shop cards terms and conditions of use here: https://www.costco.com.au/costco-shop-card-TC

You can contact us by email, phone or by filling the enquire form bellow if you have any questions.
